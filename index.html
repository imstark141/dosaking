Our Story · Hamari Kahani

Born on the Streets of MH28

From a single Tawa to Buldana's most-talked-about dosa destination — Dosa King (MH28) brings the authentic Hyderabadi dosa culture straight to your plate. Every dosa is made fresh, dripping with ghee, and served with that ek number chutney.

Known for our paper-thin crispy base, our secret Hyderabadi masala, and dosas so big they hang off the plate — this is not just food, it's a Buldana tradition.
